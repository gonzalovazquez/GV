Mission Statement

"...there is no greater joy than to have an endlessly changing horizon, for each day to have a new and different sun." ‐ Christopher McCandless

The goal of any creator is to make life a better place to live. Painters create masterpieces; composers craft heartbreaking melodies. Developers are no different, our canvas is the world and our paintbrush are zeros and ones. We create to empower people, to better life, to give back to the community. My mission is to always create software that moves the human race forward, that always gives back.

Business Model

"The key to this business is personal relationships." ‐ Dicky Fox

I believe we are very lucky to work in an industry where people want to create great things. We should take care of our work environment and our peers. I believe in a teamwork model called the Jellyfish model. Here are the main concepts of working with that approach:

  • Transparent
  • Distributed
  • Decentralized
  • Collaborative
  • Adaptive
  • Egoless

You can read more about this teamwork model.

Positive Change

"Make love, not war"
