SnapCam

A simple application that introduces the photographer to the concept of exposure.

Instruction:
  • Click on the left side of the camera and select an f-stop(example f8).
  • Click on the right side on the camera and select an aperture(example 1/100).
  • Click on the shutter button located on the top left and see the result.
  • Experiment with different combinations.
